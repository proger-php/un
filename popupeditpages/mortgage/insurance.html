Insurance

According to the Credit Agreement and mortgage loan programs offered by AO UniCredit Bank, the available property, or the one purchased (depending on the selected program), using the credit funds and subsequently pledged to the Bank, must be insured by an insurance company, selected by the Borrower; the insurance company should also comply with the Bank’s requirements, based on the mortgage insurance program under the Insurance Agreement.
