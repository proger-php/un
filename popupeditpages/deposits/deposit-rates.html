In case of early withdrawal

Client can make early withdrawal of any term deposit at any time

1. Interest rate accrues as 0.01% annualy for the actual time the funds were in the deposit.

2. The amount of the early withdrawn deposit and accrued interest return on the account from which the deposit was opened. Cash withdrawal from current account provided with commission 1% or 5%, but amount and accrued interest can be transferred to debit card, from which client can withdraw cash without commission but with daily cash withdrawal limits.

3. According to the Bank’s tariffs, no commission is charged for cash withdrawal operations from the current account of the individual client within the amount credited to this account of the early withdrawn deposit with accrued interest:
— provided that cash is deposited to this account on the day of opening the deposit and the previous working day in the amount of the placed deposit;
— placed on the basis of the client’s instructions on automatic deposit placement for a new term.

Commission compensation for deposit early withdrawal

In gratitude for clients who have opened term deposits, in case of early withdrawal of deposit we canceled the commission for cash withdrawals from your current account within the deposit amount credited to the account including the accrued interest*.

We are also willing to compensate you a commission paid for cash withdrawals from your current account for early withdrawal of term deposit*. You can fill an application in any branch of the Bank. Consideration of the application will take no more than 10 working days.

* These conditions are valid for deposits that are opened not earlier than February 1, 2011. Amount of the deposit must be placed in cash in the current account on the day or on the preceding business day when term deposit was opened, and withdrawn in cash at the Bank’s cashier on the day of deposit early withdrawal or on the next business day.
