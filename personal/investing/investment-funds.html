          APPLY

          What to choose?

          Mutual funds or deposits?

          Investments in mutual funds are totally different from bank deposits that offer only a fixed interest to customers. By purchasing a share in a mutual fund, the shareholder will receive all potential income2 generated by investments, while the asset management company will receive a fixed commission (fee) that depends on the amount of assets under its management. Due to this reason the asset management company is interested in achieving maximum return on your investments.

          Open mutual funds have greater marketability, since their shares can be redeemed on any working day. However, in case of early withdrawal of a bank deposit the depositor usually loses its interest income. In case of long-term investments, return on investments in mutual funds can be considerably greater than deposit rates offered by banks or than inflation rate.

          Mutual funds or independent investments?

          What is the difference between investments in mutual funds and independent investments in the stock market? An investor purchasing a share entrusts its funds to a professional manager as a trustee who is interested in achieving maximum increase of the value of that share. In addition, an asset management company can invest funds on more beneficial terms for its investors, since it has more funds under its management and can reduce costs connected with investments in securities. Furthermore, when you have a limited experience in investment management, investing in mutual funds allows to learn more about the stock market and prepare for independent investment activity.

          TKB Investment Partners — Premium. Equity Russia

          This fund is intended first of all for investors focused on long-term investments that reduce the dependence of results on greater volatility over a short interval of time, which is inherent in shares unlike in any other financial instrument.

          keyboard_arrow_down
          Fund’s investment policy objective

          The purpose of the investment policy of the Fund — to provide a significant increase in the estimated value of the share at the expense of investment of portfolio securities on the basis of «BestSelection». The selection of stocks held by our own analytical team is based on a thorough analysis of the market from «the bottom up». Basis of this approach is a versatile evaluation of each company of the Russian economy in isolation on a wide range of basic indication. Such approach can identify stocks that can show results higher than the overall market in the long term.

          TKB Investment Partners — Bond Russia

          This fund enables its investors to get access to investments in bonds issued by the state or by the constituent entities of the Russian Federation or by the leading Russian companies. This fund is intended first of all for investors that value stability and predictability, and are willing to protect their savings from inflation and to have an opportunity of getting a return greater than interest rates offered by banks.

          keyboard_arrow_down
          Fund’s investment policy objective

          Current income, long-term growth and safety are the Fund’s primary goals. To achieve these goals the Fund invests in high-quality debt securities issued by Russian government as well as by non-government entities with good credit quality.

          TKB Investment Partners — Bond Russia USD

          Open-ended Bond fund «TKB BNP Paribas — Bond Russia USD» enables its investors to get access to investments in foreign currency bonds issued by the leading Russian and foreign entities with good credit quality. This fund is intended first of all for investors that value stability and predictability, and are willing to protect their savings from inflation and to have an opportunity of getting a return greater than foreign currency deposits interest rates offered by banks.

          keyboard_arrow_down
          Fund’s investment policy objective

          Current income, long-term growth and safety are the Fund’s primary goals. To achieve these goals the Fund invests in high-quality Eurobonds issued by Russian and foreign entities with good credit quality.

          TKB Investment Partners — Balanced Russia

          This fund gives its shareholders an opportunity by purchasing shares in this fund to invest in a wide range of stock and debt instruments of the most reliable, stable, attractive and dynamic Russian issuers. This fund is intended first of all for investors seeking for long-term investment opportunities with a balanced ratio of return and volatility of investment portfolio.

          keyboard_arrow_down
          Fund’s investment policy objective

          The Fund aims at achieving returns that are higher than a current level of yields offered by the bond market and yet carry less risk than an equity investment. To achieve this goal the Fund invests in abroad mixture of stocks and bonds to diversify away individual securities’ volatility and credit risks.

          TKB Investment Partners — Fund of Funds Commodity Gold Tracker

          Open mutual fund of funds «TKB BNP Paribas — Gold» invests its assets in (ETF) PowerShares DB Gold Fund that reflects the dynamics of prices on gold. The advantage offered by this fund to its investors is an opportunity to invest rubles in a financial instrument, which is created and regulated in accordance with Russian laws and which price correlates to the prices on gold. Gold traditionally has been used to save and accumulate capital, especially during periods of instability.

          keyboard_arrow_down
          Fund’s investment policy objective

          The Fund invests in the PowerShares DB Gold Fund, which is based on the Deutsche Bank Liquid Commodity Index — Optimum Yield Gold Excess Return. This Index is intended to reflect the performance of gold.

          TKB Investment Partners — Equity Global

          Open-ended mixed investment fund «TKB Investment Partners – Equity Global» offers an opportunity to invest in a diversified portfolio of shares of undervalued companies from around the world. Selection of equities is based on fundamental quantitative analysis. The fund invests in equities which are underpriced in relation the value implied by the financial results of their issuers. This sets the fund apart from index funds and ETFs, where the weight of outperforming equities grows over time even as these equities become relatively expensive.

          keyboard_arrow_down
          Fund’s investment policy objective

          Increasing the value of a share by long-term investing in a diversified equity portfolio of undervalued companies from around the world. Selection of equities is based on fundamental quantitative analysis.

          Through UniCredit Bank branches

          • take your identity document with you and visit any branch of UniCredit Bank selling shares in mutual funds,
          • fill in an account opening application and a personal data sheet for purchase of shares in a mutual fund,

          Via Enter.UniCredit

          • fill in an account opening application and a personal data sheet for purchase of shares in a mutual fund at the Bank branch,
          • log in to Internet-bank and click Investments in the menu on the top,

          • fill in all required fields and confirm the transaction by entering the session key,
          • the next working day check whether the amount you paid was credited against purchase of shares in the mutual fund.

If you are not a customer of UniCredit Bank, you will have to open a current account with AO UniCredit Bank and sign a General Terms Agreement for Acceptance by AO UniCredit Bank of Bank Deposits from Individuals.
